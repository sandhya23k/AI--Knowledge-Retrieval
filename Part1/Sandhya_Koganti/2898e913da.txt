1. Wash the whole peach and cut it into small pieces.
2. Peel the whole banana and cut it into small pieces.
3. Wash a handful of whole leafy spinach and remove any stems.
4. Mince a small amount of parsley.
5. Combine all of the above ingredients in a blender.
6. Add a cup of liquid water to the blender.
7. Blend all ingredients for about 30 seconds until smooth.
8. Pour the mixture into a glass and serve immediately. Enjoy!
