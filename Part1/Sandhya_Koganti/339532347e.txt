1. Start by washing 1 cup of whole leafy spinach leaves thoroughly under cold water. 
2. In a blender, add the washed spinach leaves along with 1 cup of liquid milk. Blend them together until they form a smooth liquid.
3. Add 1 tablespoon of grain seeds such as chia, flax or hemp seeds to the blender and blend again until it becomes smooth.
4. Peel 1 whole banana and 1 whole avocado, and add it to the blender. Blend it well until it becomes smooth.
5. Finally, add 1 cup of solid ice cubes to the blender and blend again until everything is well mixed.
6. Pour the content into a serving glass, and the nutrient-rich, refreshing drink is ready to serve!
