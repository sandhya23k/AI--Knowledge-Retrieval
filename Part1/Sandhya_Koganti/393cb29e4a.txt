1. Get a clean glass and put a few scoops of creamy cream at the bottom.
2. Pour in a cup of liquid milk over the creamy cream.
3. Add 1-2 spoonful of liquid extract to the mixture.
4. Put a few teaspoons of peel sherbet on top of the creamy mixture.
5. Get a spoon and stir the mixture until it is well-blended.
6. Pour in some liquid coffee over the mixture and stir it again.
7. Add some ice if you prefer a chilled drink.
8. Mix it well again before drinking.
Enjoy your delicious concoction of creamy milk with a twist of coffee and fruity peel sherbet!
